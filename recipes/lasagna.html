Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna is a comforting and hearty dish, perfect for family dinners or gatherings. It features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese, all baked together to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
    • Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
    • Stir in the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:
    • In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
    • Whisk in the flour and cook for a minute or two until it forms a paste.
    • Gradually whisk in the milk, ensuring there are no lumps.
    • Continue to cook and whisk until the sauce thickens.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread a portion of béchamel sauce over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese and a little grated Parmesan.
    • Repeat the layers (meat sauce, noodles, béchamel, cheese) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
