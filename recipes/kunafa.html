Kunafa Recipe

Ingredients

  • sugar syrup
  • ghee
  • kunala
  • corn starch
  • sweet cheese
  • milk
  • cream

Steps

  1. Brush the bottom of your baking pan with some of the remaininf melted ghee. Take half of the knafeh and spread it evenly.
  2. Press the knafeh using the bottom of another pan or a cup or a smaller plate. Press down and along the sides of the pan.
  3. Pour the Ashta (milk pudding) mixture. Spread evenly leaving about half inch all around. Recipe for the Ashta is in the recipe card at the end of the post.
  4. If you are using the sweet cheese, then grate it and spread it evenly on top of the pudding.
  5. Add the remaining shredded knafeh , spread it evenly and press lightly with your finger tips.
  6. Spread the remaining melted ghee on top and bake until nicely browned.
